The Story

Born from the
Texas Pasture

Petal & Pasture was born from a deep love of the Texas countryside — the golden morning light over rolling pastures, the scent of wildflowers carried on a warm breeze, and the quiet charm of farmhouse living.

"Every candle is a little piece of the pasture, brought into your home."

Each sculpture is hand-poured using 100% organic beeswax that is triple filtered for exceptional clarity and a clean, even burn. We use only natural bamboo wicks — no zinc, no lead, no compromise. The result is a candle that burns beautifully and fills your space with warmth.

Our five pasture animals — the Highland Cow, Pig, Horse, Lamb, and Chicken — are each adorned with intricate hand-sculpted roses, making them as stunning to display as they are to burn.

The Process

How Every
Candle is Made

From raw beeswax to finished sculpture, every Petal & Pasture candle goes through a careful, artisan process designed to ensure the highest quality.

01

Triple-Filter the Beeswax

Raw organic beeswax is melted and filtered three times to remove all impurities, resulting in a crystal-clear pour.

02

Color & Scent

Natural pigments are blended in to achieve each of the five earthy tones. Fragrance oils are added at the precise temperature for optimal scent throw.

03

Hand Pour & Set

Each candle is poured by hand into the animal sculpture mold with a bamboo wick centered precisely for an even burn.

04

Quality Check & Package

Every candle is inspected before being placed on its grass & fence coaster and packaged — or assembled into the signature Barn Box.
