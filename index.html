Our Craft

From the Hive to Your Home

Every Petal & Pasture candle begins with 100% organic triple-filtered beeswax — the purest, cleanest wax available. No paraffin. No synthetic fragrances. No shortcuts.

We pair our beeswax with natural bamboo wicks for a clean, slow, even burn that fills your home with the warm, honey-sweet scent of pure beeswax. Each animal is hand-poured and hand-finished in small batches right here in Texas.
